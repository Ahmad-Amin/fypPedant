Pedent - An audiovisual autocorrector

Overview of Whole System

Record your video or audio with your device

Upload your audio/video to our application

Speech Recognition will convert all the audio part to extract english subtitles. It will also identify all the mispronunciations you made, or text out of order.

Pedant is a web application providing its services to regain control over your audio and video gestures. Whether its minding your pronunciation or adding dialogs you forgot, we have got you covered.

Your generated transcript will then be made editable. Edit where you made a mistake or add new sentences.

Text-to-speech algorithms will tune your audio with respect to your changings.

Video/audio will be ready to download with all the respective changes
